Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my photo?

Click on the "Upload Your Picture" button and select a photo from your device. Make sure the photo is clear and well-lit for the best AR experience.

What measurements do I need to provide?

You need to provide your height, weight, chest, waist, and hips measurements to get accurate fit recommendations for the clothes you try on.

Can I try on different outfits in AR?

Yes, once you upload your photo and enter your measurements, you can try on various outfits and see how they look on you in real-time.

Is the AR try-on feature free?

Yes, the AR try-on feature is available for free to all users. Enjoy trying on clothes virtually at no cost!
